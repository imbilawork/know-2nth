How it works

Quote-to-delivery in four steps. Every stage is handled by the same operational system the workshop uses — you and we see the same data.

1 · You request a quote
Pick products, tell us about the space, submit. No account required, no pushy email list.
2 · We reply with a priced quote
Typically within two business days. Quote includes lead time, delivery cost, and 50% deposit terms.
3 · You confirm, we build
Accept the quote, pay the 50% deposit, and the order enters our workshop queue. Progress is visible in your order tracker.
4 · Delivered, set up, guaranteed
Nationwide courier delivery with white-glove placement in Cape Town / Johannesburg. 10-year structural guarantee.
2nth.ai · About this demo

This is what ERPNext looks like with a front-end on it.

Every product, price, customer and invoice you see is real data in a private ERPNext instance running on Google Cloud in Johannesburg. When you request a quote, a Cloudflare Worker at the edge signs a request into the private ERPNext API — no direct public exposure, no CORS theatre, no SaaS middleman. The workshop team sees your order in their ERPNext desk; you see it on a page that behaves like the rest of the internet.

See the production dashboard for the workshop-side view of the same data, or the infrastructure explainer for how this is wired.
