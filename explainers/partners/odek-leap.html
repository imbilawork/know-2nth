06 · Built for South Africa

SA-hosted. POPIA-ready. Load-shedding resilient.

Data stays in South Africa. Your PostgreSQL database runs in the Johannesburg cloud region. Client data never leaves the country unless you explicitly move it. POPIA compliance is architectural, not aspirational.

Row-level security enforces the rules. Access policies are defined once at the database level and applied automatically to every query, every dashboard, every export. When a client user logs in, they see their data and nothing else — enforced by the database, not by the application hoping it filtered correctly.

Resilient to load shedding. The platform runs on cloud infrastructure with automatic failover. Queries queue and retry. Scheduled reports catch up when power returns. No analyst time is lost to infrastructure babysitting.

ZAR-native. Currency formatting, date formats, timezone handling — all configured for SAST from the start. Your client dashboards show rands and cents, not dollars with a conversion footnote.
